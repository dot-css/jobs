Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At HiringAgent, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our automated job application services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, and physical location preferences.
  • Professional Data: Your uploaded resume (PDF) and preferred job titles.
  • Google Account Data: When you connect your Gmail via Google OAuth, we receive an access token that allows us to send emails on your behalf.

2. How We Use Google User Data

Our app strictly requires the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send scope. We use this exclusively to send automated job application emails and tailored cover letters to potential employers on your behalf.

  • We do not read, modify, or delete your existing emails.
  • We do not access your contacts or use your email for any marketing purposes.
  • Your Google OAuth tokens are securely transmitted to our backend (n8n) solely for the purpose of executing your daily application runs.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your resume and personal details are used exclusively to:

  • Generate AI-written, personalized cover letters tailored to specific job postings.
  • Fill out necessary details required by employers.
  • Send you daily progress reports regarding the applications submitted.

4. Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is never shared with third-party marketers, data brokers, or advertisers. The only entities that receive your data are the prospective employers to whom applications are sent on your behalf.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your resume and preferences only for as long as your subscription is active. If you cancel your subscription, you may request the complete deletion of your data from our servers by contacting our support team via WhatsApp or email.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and resume files from unauthorized access. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hiringagentapp@gmail.com.
